Rules and Procedures
Welcome to HuskyHunt!
We have a few simple rules.
  • Be respectful of others.
  • Keep profanity to a minimum.
  • Be safe.
Messaging a seller:

To message a seller, click on the item and type your message in the "message seller" text box. Our email service will message the seller and share your MTU email address with the seller. The seller can contact you via email to dicuss more.

Selling an item

Once your item is posted on the site, check your MTU inbox for messages from potential buyers. To message a potential buyer back, message their MTU email address provided in the email.

Please be aware when you message a seller your MTU email address will be shared with that seller. We do not guarantee any reliablity
or accuracy regarding items or rides. Any action taken outside of the HuskyHunt website (such as through emailing) is strictly at your own risk.

For issues regarding the website, please contact our development team at huskyhunt-l@mtu.edu
